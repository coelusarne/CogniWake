Download the image from the google drive: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1fp22YV6iv-l65GfR7GNjxbW5kU8ssPuj/view?usp=sharing
Extract the archive.
Use extracted archive to flash your SD card.

Note: The alarm service included on the image has a bug where the alarm is triggered if there is 0 days selected.

To replace the AlarmService on the device:
ssh into cogniwake@{ip-in-network-box}

in the CogniWake directory:
Locate backend/services/AlarmService.py
replace it with the version in this repository

in a terminal, run:
sudo pkill -9 uvicorn

and immediately after: 
sudo systemctl restart fastapi

Alternatively, you can also reboot the device via the menus.
